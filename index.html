Representation of People of Color at the Oscars 1927 - 2019

The Oscars have recently come under fire for a lack of representation of people of color (POC), with the phrase "Oscars so White" being coined. When looking at all time Oscar nominees, it is clear that the overwhelming majority of Oscar nominees have been White. This has clear downstream effects, as fewer Oscar nominations also translates to fewer Oscar wins.
